# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

SparkyBot is a community tool built for Guild Wars 2 WvW players. We're committed to providing a welcoming and respectful experience for everyone — whether you're filing a bug report, suggesting a feature, or using the tool in your guild's Discord.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in issue discussions and pull requests
- Provide helpful context when reporting bugs (version, logs, steps to reproduce)
- Respect the time of maintainers — this is a volunteer-run project

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks in any project space
- Posting others' private information (API keys, tokens, webhook URLs, real names)
- Spam, trolling, or deliberately unhelpful contributions

## AI-Generated Content Disclaimer

SparkyBot includes an optional AI Fight Commentary feature that sends fight statistics to a **user-configured third-party AI provider** (such as OpenAI, MiniMax, Google Gemini, or others) and posts the AI-generated response to Discord and/or Twitch.

**Important:**

- **AI output is not authored, reviewed, or endorsed by SparkyBot or its maintainers.** The commentary is generated entirely by the third-party AI provider based on fight data and the user's configured system prompt.
- **Users are responsible for their own AI configuration.** The system prompt is fully editable — users control the tone, language, and behavior of the AI output posted to their channels.
- **SparkyBot's default prompt encourages competitive WvW banter** (roasting PUGs, mocking enemy compositions, hype commentary). This is intended as lighthearted gaming humor, not personal attacks on real individuals.
- **AI models are unpredictable.** Despite prompt instructions, third-party AI providers may occasionally generate content that is unexpected, inaccurate, or inappropriate. SparkyBot cannot filter, moderate, or guarantee AI output.
- **If AI commentary produces inappropriate content in your channel**, you can:
  - Edit the system prompt in Settings → AI to adjust the tone
  - Disable AI commentary entirely by unchecking "Enable AI Fight Analysis"
  - Delete the message from your Discord/Twitch channel as you would any other message

**The SparkyBot project and its maintainers accept no liability for content generated by third-party AI providers.** By enabling the AI commentary feature, users acknowledge that they are responsible for the output posted to their own channels.

## Reporting Issues

- **Code of Conduct violations in project spaces:** Open an issue or contact SimpleHonors directly
- **Security vulnerabilities:** See [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) — do not open a public issue
- **Problematic AI output:** This is between you, your system prompt, and your AI provider — not a SparkyBot bug. However, if you find a prompt improvement that reduces unwanted output, we welcome the suggestion as a feature request.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all SparkyBot project spaces: GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and any official communication channels. It does not govern the content of fight reports or AI commentary posted to users' own Discord servers or Twitch channels — those are the responsibility of the channel operators.

## Enforcement

Violations of this Code of Conduct in project spaces may result in comments being removed, issues being locked, or users being blocked from the repository at the maintainers' discretion.

This Code of Conduct is adapted for the specific needs of the SparkyBot project. It is not based on a standard template.
